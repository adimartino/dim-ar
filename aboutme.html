Why me?

Who am I? Why choose me as your UX/UI designer?

I'm Agus Di Martino, a UX/UI designer – the guy who turns projects into experiences where everything just flows (like finding an empty checkout line at the supermarket). If your app or website were a party, I’d make sure every guest felt welcomed and knew exactly where the drinks were.

It’s not just about making things "pretty". For me, good design is like a well-made playlist: it keeps the rhythm, engages people, and makes them want to stay. My go-to tool is Figma, and I always bring a few memes to lighten the mood – because work doesn’t always need to be serious, right?

So, if you’re looking for a design that not only looks great but is also functional and drama-free, I’m your guy.

What am I best at?

  • UX Researching
  • Interface Design (UI)
  • UX Writing
