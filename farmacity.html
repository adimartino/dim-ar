Farmacity

Farmacity new customer benefits program

For the past 4 years, I have been working as a UX/UI designer at Farmacity, leading end-to-end design processes across multiple digital products. I created and maintained the company’s design system, redesigned the mobile app and website, and collaborated closely with developers and stakeholders to deliver scalable, high-quality user experiences that supported the company’s digital transformation.

View Case Study

Farmacity’s digital products had grown without a unified strategy, causing inconsistent experiences, duplicated components, and usability issues. The challenge: build a scalable design system, improve UX flows, and create a visually coherent interface.

Problems to solve

  • Lack of design system: different teams building inconsistent UIs.
  • Complex user flows: checkout and appointment booking had high drop-off rates.
  • Non-responsive legacy components caused issues on mobile devices.
  • Need for modernizing visual identity to match Farmacity’s brand evolution.

I led the UX/UI initiatives end-to-end: Research, design, prototyping, testing, and delivering production-ready components to development teams. I collaborated with product managers, developers, and marketing to ensure a cohesive product strategy.

The process

1. UX Research & Auditing
Conducted user testing, heatmaps analysis, and heuristic evaluations to identify major pain points in the customer journey.

2. Information Architecture
Reorganized navigation and simplified purchase flows, reducing steps and cognitive load for users.

3. Design System Creation
Built a scalable system of components with Figma libraries, documentation, and guidelines for developers.

4. UI Redesign
Redesigned the mobile app and e-commerce platform with modern patterns, improved accessibility, and a consistent look-and-feel across all products.

“Consistency is not just visual. A good design system aligns people, processes, and technology to create seamless user experiences.”

Design System Colors

UX/UI Design Approach

Farmacity’s redesign followed a systematic UX approach with continuous testing and iteration. Using the design system, we accelerated development cycles and improved cross-team collaboration.

  • Design System: Created 200+ components with tokens for scalability.
  • User Flows: Reduced checkout process from 6 steps to 3.
  • Accessibility: Applied WCAG standards, improving contrast and readability.
  • Mobile-first: Redesigned navigation for touch-friendly interactions.

This approach resulted in consistent design language and a 20% increase in user satisfaction scores based on surveys and NPS data.

Results & Impact

The redesign and design system implementation had measurable impact:

  • Consistency: Unified look-and-feel across web and mobile platforms.
  • Efficiency: Reduced design-to-dev handoff time by 40%.
  • Performance: Optimized components led to a 25% faster app load time.
  • User Engagement: Increased repeat purchases and improved conversion rates.

This project demonstrates how a strong UX/UI foundation and design system can scale a product and improve the experience for millions of users.
